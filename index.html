About
This project is satire. We examine what happens when AI translates animal communication into human-understandable terms: one vocalization becomes hundreds of labels (emotion, alarm, greeting, hunger…). The map is a digital graphic of that reduction. Each dot is a reductive translation; the center is the raw signal. The animals didn't confirm any of it. We flattened complexity into words. You decide: simplification or loss.
